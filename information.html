Policy

Club Mission

1. The club is committed to promoting the development of fishing, advocating a civilized and pleasant lifestyle, and advocating the positive and enterprising spirit of honing one's will, exercising one's physique and challenging oneself.

2. The club is committed to environmental and ecological protection, and advocates the harmonious development of man and nature.

3. The club will also be committed to creating an atmosphere of love, equality, tolerance and mutual assistance among members, and advocating self-help, sharing and scientific knowledge seeking behavior.

4. The club advocates the spirit of helping each other.

Way to become a Club Members

Anyone who loves fishing activities, loves nature and is in good health is eligible to become a member of this club.

Complete the application registration form and submit it to become a member.

Member's obligation

1. Establish a positive and healthy concept of environmental protection, and take the initiative to consciously implement it in action, to influence everyone around, to be a green outdoor sportsman.

2. Concerned about the club's activities and developments, maintain regular contact with the club.

3. Contact with the club, assist in the promotion of activities, membership development, business contacts and other things that can be done.

4. Share valuable fishing information with the club, such as photos of fishing activities and routes, and fishing tips, to enrich the club's data center.

5. Actively participate in the club's members' meetings and gatherings, and provide advice and suggestions on the club.
